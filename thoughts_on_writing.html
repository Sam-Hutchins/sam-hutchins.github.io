Notes to myself on writing

  • You're not writing for you: Writing should be done for the readers, and as many of them as possible.
  • Remember what the point is: The point of writing is simply to get information from your head into someone elses head. Your chosen medium is writing.
  • Keep it simple: Using unnecessary complex words is just a burden on the reader. Do it for effect an never without thinking it through.
  • Edit Ruthlessly: Writing is editing, editing is cutting.
